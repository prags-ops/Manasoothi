What is Anger?

Anger is a normal but powerful emotion that ranges from mild irritation to intense rage. It motivates us to respond to perceived threats and frustrations.

Recognizing your emotions and practicing healthy coping mechanisms can reduce the negative impacts of anger.

Ways to Manage Anger

  • Practice deep breathing exercises daily to calm your mind and body.
  • Engage in mindfulness and meditation practices.
  • Take breaks from stressful situations to regain composure.
  • Talk to a trusted person or counselor when feeling overwhelmed.
