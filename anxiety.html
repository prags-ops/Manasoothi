What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease about uncertain outcomes. It is a typical human emotion that can sometimes become overwhelming.

Learning to recognize and manage anxiety is important for maintaining your well-being.

Ways to Manage Anxiety

  • Practice deep breathing and progressive muscle relaxation.
  • Engage in physical exercise to reduce stress levels.
  • Challenge negative thoughts with positive affirmations.
  • Consider professional mental health support when necessary.

Breathing Exercises for Anxiety Relief

Practice these simple breathing techniques regularly to calm anxiety:

  1. Sit or lie down comfortably with your back straight.
  2. Place one hand on your belly and the other on your chest.
  3. Inhale deeply through your nose, feeling your abdomen rise.
  4. Exhale slowly through pursed lips, releasing tension.
  5. Try the 4-7-8 technique: breathe in for 4 seconds, hold 7 seconds, breathe out for 8 seconds.
  6. Repeat for 4-5 cycles or longer as needed.
